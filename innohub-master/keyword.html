What is Keyword Targeting?

Keyword targeting is the practice of identifying and using the right keywords to enhance your website’s visibility in search engines. It forms the backbone of every successful digital marketing strategy.

Why is Keyword Targeting Important?

  • Boosts conversions by matching user intent with your content.
